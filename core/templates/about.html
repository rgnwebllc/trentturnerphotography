About Trent Turner Photography

Empowering portraits that celebrate confidence, style, and individuality.

Hi, I'm Trent.

I specialize in capturing bold, authentic portraits that highlight who you are — not who the world expects you to be. My studio is a space where confidence is celebrated, personality shines, and no one needs permission to feel powerful.

Whether you're booking a solo session, a creative shoot, or a brand portrait, my mission is the same: to make you feel confident in your own skin and walk away with images that tell your story.

“Confidence isn't something you find — it's something you unlock. My camera just helps you see it.”

Trent Turner

My Journey

📸 2018 – The First Shot

Started photography with a borrowed DSLR and a deep passion for storytelling. My first shoots were for friends and family in Hobart, IN.

📷 2020 – Style & Growth

Refined my editing and lighting techniques. I began shaping a bold, editorial-inspired style that caught attention locally.

🏆 2023 – Studio Launch

Opened my own home studio and started offering private, styled shoots. Sessions filled up fast, thanks to referrals and client trust.

🚀 Today – Creative Confidence

Now serving Northwest Indiana and beyond — helping people step in front of the camera with purpose, confidence, and power.
